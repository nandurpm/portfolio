Design Principles I Follow

User-Centered Design

Every design decision is made with the user in mind, ensuring the final product meets their needs and expectations.

Consistency

Consistent use of design elements, patterns, and interactions across all screens and components.

Designs that are accessible to all users, including those with disabilities, following WCAG guidelines.

Designs that work seamlessly across all devices and screen sizes, from mobile to desktop.

Innovation

Always exploring new design trends and techniques while maintaining timeless design principles.
