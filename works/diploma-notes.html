Diploma Notes

A separate GitHub Pages-ready study platform for Kerala Polytechnic and Diploma students.

What It Is

A focused study resource product for Diploma students.

Diploma Notes is an independent educational website with its own repository, theme, and future custom domain. It is built to organize Kerala Diploma study resources by revision, department, semester, subject, and material type.

01

About and Purpose

Help Diploma students quickly find official syllabus pages, lesson sections, model question papers, previous papers, and study materials without digging through scattered links.

02

Features

Department filters, subject cards, dynamic syllabus links, lessons access, generated model question paper URLs, first-year resources, and clean responsive navigation.

03

How to Use

Open the Diploma Notes website, choose a department or material section, search by course code or subject, then use the subject action buttons for syllabus, lessons, or question papers.

04

Future Scope

Add more branches, downloadable local notes, previous question paper collections, student contribution workflows, and a separate custom domain for the product.
